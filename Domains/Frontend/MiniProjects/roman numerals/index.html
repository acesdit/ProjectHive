How Roman Numerals Work

Roman numerals use combinations of letters from the Latin alphabet (I, V, X, L, C, D, M). Only numbers from 1 to 3999 are supported in this converter.

Examples

4 → IV
9 → IX
58 → LVIII
1994 → MCMXCIV
2023 → MMXXIII
